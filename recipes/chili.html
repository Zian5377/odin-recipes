Chili Recipe

Delicious Homemade Chili

This recipe is simple and easy to follow, and in no time at all you and your family will be enjoying a nice, hot bowl of fresh chili, guaranteed to warm the whole family up during the colder months

When it comes to making chili, nothing beats using fresh veggies that guarantee your dish will be fragrant and full of flavor! So buckle up, we're taking a trip to flavor town. all rights to flavor town belong to Guy Fieri... Probably

Ingredients

  • 1LB of Ground Meat (Beef Prefered)
  • Half of white onion
  • One bell pepper
  • Chili Seasoning
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic (freshly minced)
  • One can of diced tomatoes
  • One can of Light Red Kidney Beans
  • One can of Dark Red Kidney Beans
  • One can of chili beans
  • One large can of crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and bell pepper to one inch or smaller pieces.
  2. Brown the meat in your slow cooker, and once it is fully cooked add in diced onions and bell pepper.
  3. After letting the onion and bell pepper soften for roughly five minutes, add in your three cans of beans, the diced tomatoes, and the crushed tomatoes.
  4. Add in chili seasoning and chili powder to taste, put on the lid and simmer for two hours, stirring occasionally
